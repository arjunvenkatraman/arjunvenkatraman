A ronin: a warrior who serves no master, but is not a master yet himself. Literally, "wave man."

I've decided to leave secure employment.

Leaving feels like departing from the East India Trading Company to join the crew of what looks like the Black Pearl. I contemplate whether losing the institutional backing will undermine my marketability. I wonder, neurotically, whether my future wife might leave me — then catch myself, noting I am not even married. I am imagining worst-case scenarios preemptively, "so that if something bad does happen, I can say 'Heh! I knew that was coming.'"

Two possible futures: achieving success with the reflection that I took the road less travelled — or sitting in the GuruShree bar on the road to nowhere, lamenting former security.
