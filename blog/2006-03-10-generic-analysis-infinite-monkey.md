My consistent laziness through engineering — studying only a week before exams — paradoxically proved beneficial. Rather than mastering Computer Science & Engineering exclusively, my lack of specialization became an asset.

The worst thing you can ever do to yourself is to specialize in a given area. Chaos theory holds that complex systems remain unpredictable. Engineering graduates in India's IT industry command high salaries regardless of branch, because employers value what someone has the potential to learn and do in the future more than existing expertise.
