**Goal:** Connect Apple to Orange in as roundabout a way as possible.

Apple was founded by two Steves → Steve Waugh is an Australian cricketer → the Kookaburra is an Australian bird featured in an old Indian Boy Scout song → Baden Powell founded the Indian Boy Scouts and associated with Annie Besant → Besant Nagar beach in Chennai is named after her → Chennai is a major port → port wine originates from Portugal → Asterix comes from France → "asterisk" relates to Asterix → Asterix is open-source IP PBX software → IP telephony disrupts telecom companies like Orange.

---

*Comment from Avinash: port wine actually comes from Porto, Portugal, not France.*
