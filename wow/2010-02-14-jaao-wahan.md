To sense after sensing, to accomplish after accomplishing, to know after knowing — none of this is worthy of being called knowing.

Develop three abilities: acting before something exists, sensing before activation, perceiving before emergence. With these capacities, nothing is sensed but is comprehended, nothing is undertaken without response, nowhere does one go without benefit.

— *The Book of Balance and Harmony*

---

जाओ वहाँ न जाने कहाँ, लाओ वह न जाने क्या

*(Go somewhere unknown, bring back something unknowable)*

— Russian folklore
