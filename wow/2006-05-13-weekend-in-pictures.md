Friday night at work, analyzing an unintelligible stream of statistical data while chatting online with colleagues.

Saturday morning: early phone call at 9:30 AM requiring a network change. Then an expedition into Bristol armed with a new digital camera.

A red brick warehouse along Bristol Harbour. Once, these warehouses stocked the tobacco that filled the cigarettes that most of the Commonwealth smoked — brands like Wills, and a cigarette brand named Bristol itself. Now they've been repurposed as office buildings. The end of a hazy era.
