*(Opens with lines from a Pink Floyd composition about loss and absence.)*

What happens if you die and then discover that you're in a parallel universe exactly similar except that you can't even die because you are already dead?

When you're trapped in constrictive circumstances, you grin and bear it — until the moment arrives. But when that moment comes and you break free, the consequences are severe. Because it's a lead role. Everyone's looking.

And "fucked" takes on a whole new meaning when your actions attract that kind of scrutiny.
