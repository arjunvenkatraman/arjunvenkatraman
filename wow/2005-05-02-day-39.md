My two flatmates and I have lived together for four years. We're experiencing parting anxiety as we prepare for life changes 39 days away. We recently drove 100 kilometers just to sit at a roadside tea stall together, acknowledging that such "adolescent" behavior won't be possible much longer once work begins.

Moving from college to work feels like the fish in the river, heading very purposefully out to sea. The school-to-college transition felt like jumping into cold river waters. This one is different.

The lab is uncomfortably hot. Poor ventilation. The engineering college's lack of thoughtful design. "Lack of thought" is worse than any post-graduation anxiety.

Despite attending a second string place, we have done and seen more than a lot of people manage in a lifetime. Three cities are going to be seriously messed up once we disperse.

Tomorrow is a fresh start.
