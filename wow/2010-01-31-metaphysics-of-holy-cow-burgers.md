"Holy Cows" are certainties — transport mechanisms moving between fixed points. Each location has its appropriate set of cows. When a Holy Cow loses relevance at a location, it accepts being "Burgered" — a transformation leading to moksha.

The greatness of humans is that we can point at J Random Cow and yell "Holy Cow!" and then be thrilled with wherever that gets us.
