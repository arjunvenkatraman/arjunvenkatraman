title: "Of Facts and Feelings"
date: 2010-09-13
source: https://arjunvenkatraman.blogspot.com/2010/09/of-facts-and-feelings.html
---

Propositions merely express facts about the world and are entirely devoid of value.

Aesthetic and ethical judgments transcend logical language — they aren't factual statements. Propositions are stepping stones to understanding, which one must eventually surpass: "He must transcend these propositions, and then he will see the world aright."

Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent.

— Wittgenstein
