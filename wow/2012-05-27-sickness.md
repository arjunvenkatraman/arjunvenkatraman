---
title: "Sickness"
date: 2012-05-27
source: https://arjunvenkatraman.blogspot.com/2012/05/sickness.html
---

It is sickness to be obsessed with winning, it is sickness to be obsessed with using martial arts, it is sickness to be obsessed with demonstrating what you have learned, it is sickness to be obsessed with offense, and it is sickness to be obsessed with defense. It is also sickness to be obsessed with getting rid of sickness. To always be thinking about this is sickness. Since all of these are sickness of the mind, you must put the mind right by getting rid of such fixations, and make it your work to do this.

— Yagyu Munenori, *The Book of Family Traditions*
