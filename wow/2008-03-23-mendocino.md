Waves driven by a crazy sergeant major, who believes that if he has enough men, they can overwhelm any enemy — crashing against rocky coastal formations, persistently, forever.

Cool breezes across meadows. Chardonnay. Sheep.

The value of companionship alongside freedom from obligations and schedules. The now-ness and the here-ness of the moment.

Some memories genuinely deserve to be preserved.
