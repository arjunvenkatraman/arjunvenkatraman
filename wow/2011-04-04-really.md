Anyone feel a tremor just now?

About an hour later — if cross referenced agreement is the only proof of reality then unless it was a group hallucination, the twitter stream would bear out that what I speak is the absolute truth.

But what if I'm a figment of someone else's imagination? A character in a dream, conjured up in an instant as a hacked up, steroid-ed version of a repressed childhood memory.

How do I know this isn't literally the first instant of my existence, with a full set of artificial memories implanted into my mind — memories of a life that never happened, a past that never was?
