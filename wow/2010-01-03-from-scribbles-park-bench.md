---
title: "From Scribbles: A Park Bench"
date: 2010-01-03
source: https://arjunvenkatraman.blogspot.com/2010/01/from-scribbles-park-bench.html
---

A park bench, behind a stack of pipes, which my girlfriend tells me are used for phone cables. Cigarette smoke. A cow. Orange flowers I cannot name.

Thoughts rush through my brain, each vying for attention, while I contemplate writing as rain threatens. There is something absurd about a mortal defying fate itself — remaining at a bench in the face of weather.

The mortal does not fear God for he knows that God is his own creation. But he fears himself.

Then the phone rings.

Nothing remains except a few squiggles on a damp piece of paper.
