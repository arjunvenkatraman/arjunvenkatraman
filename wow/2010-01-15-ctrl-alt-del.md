I hit rock bottom at the start of the year. I realized I had built my life around trying to appear successful while feeling fundamentally alone. I attempted various strategies to become more lovable, likeable and successful — but found these approaches left me untouched, unmoved and uninspired.

Rather than considering escape, I decided to test whether the people claiming to depend on me actually did. This led to vulnerable conversations with my parents, who demonstrated unconditional support, and later with my mentor, where authentic connection replaced performative friendship.

I made four promises:

1. Give my word only when genuinely moved and inspired
2. Play with full commitment
3. Maintain open communication without fear-based withholding
4. Operate consistently from love

Personal power comes from saying exactly what's so and what's not, while remaining willing to accept consequences — regardless of outcomes.
